Vision

To realise an equitable and just society as well as promote national development.

Mission

To develop graduates to their full human potential (human consciousness, human conduct), who will be committed to national development and global well-being in a collaborative manner with all concerned agencies.

Objective

1.To develop Right Understanding By sharing the understanding of what to do (Value Education)
2.To develop Right Skills by teaching how to do (Technical Education)
3.To Practice Right living during the period of education.
a.Living in relationship with human being leading to mutual happiness
 b.Living in relationship with rest-of-nature leading to mutual prosperity
